    Scientific Method.

    What is the scientific method?

    The scientific method is a method of investigation used to figure out questions trough experimentation.

    Scientific Method Steps
    Observations

    Ask questions about something. And think about it.

    Gather Information

    Research about the topic, try finding out the answer to your question, or document yourself to understand more.

    State a hypothesis

    Tentative explanation based on observations and research.

    Experimentation

    Experimentate your hypothesis, and try proving it with the experiment. If it doesnt prove it, re-state your hypothesis, else go to the next step.

    Analyze Data and Make Conclusions

    Analyze the data and make conclusions, eventually you can publish the results.

    Make new questions

    Make new questions with the information you have!
